Bone Collector

How to Play

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four dogs below. By clicking on a dog you will add a specific amount of bones to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, if your total score goes above the random number, you lose.

The value of each dog is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the values of the dogs will change.
